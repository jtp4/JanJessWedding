Jan

Jan tenPas IV was born in Lynchburg, VA. He moved to Richmond when he was a toddler where he grew up with two wonderful parents, Jan and Sandi, and an awesome older sister, Jackie. Jan attended Longwood University and later graduated with his Masters from George Mason University. He is the fourth Jan tenPas, so you might hear his family call him by his childhood nickname “Johnny”. Jan is also going to marry the most beautiful and amazing woman in the World.

HOW WE MET

Jan and Jess met the old fashioned way, on the Internet. After e-mailing just one time, Jan boldly asked to skip all the back and forth and meet for dinner. Jessica graciously accepted, not knowing that would be the first date with her soul mate. They met at Geranio Ristorante, a little Italian restaurant in Old Town Alexandria. They talked about their jobs and interests, growing more interested as the conversation continued. There was an instant connection. So Jessica was surprised when Jan asked for the check right after dinner. She was bummed, thinking that they had really hit things off. Don’t worry though, Jan knew what he was doing. Having a keen lay of land, he asked Jessica to join him for Sangria at a local Tapas bar. They watched basketball and found out that they shared so much in common. Jan walked Jessica to her car and hugged her goodbye, like a gentlemen. Each drove away that night excited for the potential of this brand new relationship. Of course as we all know now, that was the night that Jan and Jess met their best friend for life.
