Jan

Jan tenPas IV was born in Lynchburg, VA. He moved to Richmond when he was a toddler where he grew up with two wonderful parents, Jan and Sandi, and an awesome older sister, Jackie. Jan attended Longwood University and later graduated with his Masters from George Mason University. He is the fourth Jan tenPas, so you might hear his family call him by his childhood nickname “Johnny”. Jan is also going to marry the most beautiful and amazing woman in the World.

HOW WE MET

Jan tenPas was a swinging bachelor in the big city of Alexandria. He was living the life of a high tech Software Developer and loving every minute. He went on many great adventures and led a truly blessed life with great friends and a loving family. Alas something was missing...well not something but someone he thought. She must be out there, and he was determined to find her. After searching for years his hopes were waining. Finally, he turned to something he knew, technology, to find his one true Match. After several less than successful dates, our young hero thought that perhaps he was meant to wonder this world alone. Wary of searching, he prayed to finally meet the one that could share his life of nerdy fun and unhinged adventure.

Jessica Naquin was ready for someone that shared her interests, determination, and adventourous spirit. New to online dating, she received a message from a rather forward young gentleman, who asked her to skip all the online protocols and just meet. She boldly agreed and met him in Old Town Alexandria at a local restaurant. After an hour of riveting conversation, Jess was shocked when Jan asked for the check right after dinner. Surely he felt the spark as well…was he really going to leave so early? Standing up and dissapointed, she was releaved when he asked her to join him for a glass of Sangria at place she "had to try". The two stayed up way to late talking, laughing, and lighting the spark that would lead to their engagement. That my friends was the start of Jess and Jan’s happily ever after.
